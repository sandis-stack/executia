Modern systems manage processes.
They do not verify execution.

01

§ 03 — The Solution

A protocol for verified execution.

◎

Responsibility Assignment

◈

Verification Layer

§ 04 — The Protocol

EXECUTIA Protocol

A governance protocol ensuring that decisions cannot be executed without verified responsibility.

P.01

Decision Registration

Every institutional decision must be registered within the execution system before any action can begin.

P.02

Responsibility Assignment

A responsible actor must be assigned before execution begins. No assignment — no execution. No exception.

P.03

Verification Gate

Execution cannot proceed without confirmation from the responsible authority. The system enforces this structurally.

P.04

Traceable Execution

All execution steps are recorded and auditable. The complete chain cannot be altered, expunged, or explained away.

P.05

Accountability Persistence

Responsibility cannot disappear during the execution process. It is permanent, unambiguous, and cryptographically linked.
