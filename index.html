HOW IT WORKS

Every payment is checked before execution.

Every payment is routed through EXECUTIA before execution, validated in real time, and either approved, blocked, or sent for review.

PAYMENT REQUEST → VALIDATION → APPROVED / BLOCKED / REVIEW
— APPROVED — payment proceeds
— BLOCKED — payment does not happen
— REVIEW — payment is held for operator review

No validation = no controlled payment.
